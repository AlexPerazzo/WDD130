What Is This Website?

This website hightlights key stats from different local Smash Brothers' players that makes ranking players easier! It's focused on the Eastern Idaho Smash Scene.

Who Is This For?

This website is meant for all sorts of people! If you just enjoy looking at statistics, are on a Player Rankings Panel, are a top player, this website is a great place to be!

How Can This Help Me?

This is a great resource in many different ways. If you want to compare players, look at weaknesses to shore up, or just enjoy stats, all the important stats are laid out for you!

What Stats Do You Display?

We focus in on what we believe are the most important statistics. Head to Heads versus other top players, notable losses and wins, and a layout of all tournament results.
